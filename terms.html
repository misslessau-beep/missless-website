Unused cards accumulate month to month and may be used at any time during your subscription year. Your card balance resets to zero on the anniversary of your original signup date each year.

We will send you a reminder email 30 days before your anniversary date so you have time to schedule any remaining cards before they expire. Any balance remaining on your anniversary date is forfeited and has no monetary value.

On cancellation of your subscription, any accumulated card balance expires immediately and is not refundable, except where required by Australian Consumer Law.
