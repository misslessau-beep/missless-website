Getting started

How does Missless work? ▾
You sign up, choose a plan, and add the people you want to remember — just their name, occasion and date. A few weeks before each occasion we contact you to confirm their delivery address and card message. Then we design, print and post a beautiful card directly to them. You do nothing else.
How long does it take to set up? ▾
About 2 minutes. Sign up, choose a plan, and add your first person. Their address and card message can be confirmed closer to the occasion — we'll remind you well in advance.
Do I need to do anything each year? ▾
Almost nothing. About 4 weeks before each occasion we'll email you to confirm the delivery address is still current and give you the option to update the message. If nothing has changed, you can confirm in one click.

Cards & designs

Can I choose the card design? ▾
Yes. When we contact you ahead of each occasion you can choose from our current designs. We add new designs every season.
Can I write my own message inside? ▾
Absolutely. You can provide your own personal message or let us write something heartfelt on your behalf — at no extra charge.
What quality are the cards? ▾
Cards are printed at 300dpi on premium thick card stock. They look and feel like cards from a boutique shop — the kind people keep, not throw away.
Will my recipient know it came from Missless? ▾
No. Cards are sent from you. Your secret is safe with us.

Delivery

Is postage included? ▾
Yes — Australian postage is always included in every plan and every pay-per-card order. No hidden fees.
Can you send cards internationally? ▾
Yes. International delivery is available for an additional $5.99 per card on all plans including Unlimited.
How early will the card arrive? ▾
We post Australian cards 28 days before the occasion, so they typically arrive 3–10 days early. For international addresses we post 42 days before.
What if the card doesn't arrive? ▾
Contact us at hello@missless.com.au. After a waiting period of 10 business days (Australian) or 21 business days (international), we reprint and resend at no charge.

Plans & billing

Can I change plans? ▾
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
Can I cancel any time? ▾
Yes. Cancel any time with no penalty. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period.
Do unused cards roll over? ▾
No. Unused allowances on Essential and Family plans do not roll over. The Unlimited plan has no monthly quota — only the 20 card fair use limit applies.
Can I pause my subscription? ▾
Yes — email hello@missless.com.au and we'll pause for up to 3 months. Available once per 12 month period.
