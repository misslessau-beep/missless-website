Questions
About This plan.
How does it work without a subscription? ▾
You pay for one card upfront. We'll contact you to confirm the delivery address, card design and message. Then we print and post it — simple as that.
Can I upgrade to a subscription after? ▾
Yes — sign up for any plan at any time. Your pay-per-card order is completely separate.
How far in advance do I need to order? ▾
We recommend ordering at least 4 weeks before the occasion for Australian addresses, and 5 weeks for international.
What if the card doesn't arrive? ▾
Contact us. After a waiting period we will reprint and resend at no charge. See our Refund Policy for full details.
Is this a one-time charge? ▾
Yes. Pay per card is a single payment of $19.99. No recurring charges, no hidden fees.
✓ Cards roll over — never lose what you've paid for
Unused cards accumulate throughout your subscription year and reset on your annual signup anniversary. We'll remind you 30 days before so you always have time to use them. Note: your balance expires immediately if you cancel — we recommend using remaining cards before cancelling.
Need more cards in a busy month?
Your accumulated balance covers busy months. If you ever run out entirely, we'll notify you — add extras at $19.99 each or upgrade your plan. No automatic charges. See FAQ →
