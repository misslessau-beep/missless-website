Shipping & Delivery
Last updated: 9 April 2026

Simple rule: Add occasions at least 4 weeks before the date and your card will arrive on time. AU postage always included.

Australia — postage always included

All plans include standard Australian postage. Cards are posted via Australia Post standard mail.

Delivery timeframes within Australia

  • Metro areas (Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth): 2–5 business days
  • Regional areas: 4–8 business days
  • Remote areas: 6–14 business days

We post cards approximately 28 days before your occasion date to ensure on-time delivery.

International delivery

International delivery is available for an additional $5.99 per card on all plans including Premium.

International delivery timeframes

  • New Zealand: 5–10 business days
  • United Kingdom: 10–20 business days
  • United States & Canada: 10–20 business days
  • Europe: 12–20 business days
  • Rest of world: 14–30 business days

For international deliveries we recommend providing occasion details at least 5 weeks before the date.

When We Post Your Card

We post Australian cards 28 days before your occasion, and international cards 42 days before. We contact you approximately 4 weeks before posting to confirm the address and message.

Tracking

Standard letters are not tracked. Contact us if you require tracked posting — this can be arranged for an additional fee.

Lost Cards

If your card hasn't arrived after 10 business days (AU) or 21 business days (international) from the expected date, contact us and we will reprint and resend at no charge.

Change of Address

Update addresses at least 28 days before posting. We cannot redirect cards once posted.
